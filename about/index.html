About SFDevs

Nestled in the middle of the Heartland you’ll find Sioux Falls, SD. Sioux Falls has a booming developer community, we’re also known as the “Silicon Prairie.”

The Sioux Falls Developers Group is open to developers in the area. Each month we bring in speakers to present on a wide variety of software development topics. Once a year, we put on a developer centric conference, South Dakota Code Camp.
